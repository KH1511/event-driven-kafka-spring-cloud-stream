***Starting the consumer for receiving messages in the topic : T1
docker exec --interactive --tty ccn-kafla-broker kafka-console-consumer --bootstrap-server broker:9092 --topic T1

****Starting consumer and receiving all messages
docker exec --interactive --tty ccn-kafla-broker kafka-console-consumer --bootstrap-server broker:9092 --topic T1 --from-beginning

****Start Producer
docker exec --interactive --tty ccn-kafla-broker kafka-console-producer --bootstrap-server broker:9092 --topic T1
